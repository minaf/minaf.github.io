About me

I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (Automatic Control and Electronics) from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, University of Sarajevo.

My MSc. Thesis was on "Rapidly exploring random vines", and was supervised by Dr. Adnan Tahirovic.

On September 2017, I began my Ph.D. studies at KTH under the supervision of Håkan Hjalmarsson. My main interests are among the areas of system identification, signal processing, and control theory.
