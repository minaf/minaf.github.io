Research and Publications

In my MSc. Thesis, I worked on Motion Planning in Configuration Spaces with Narrow Passages. During my stay at EPFL, I worked on the media transition in salamander robots to improve search and rescue capabilities. I currently do research on adaptive control, dual control, and reinforcement learning, i.e., carrying out learning and control simultaneously.
